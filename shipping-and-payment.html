Shipping & Payment

Shipping

We offer FREE tracked delivery and shipping on all orders, UK and worldwide.



Where is my order coming from?

Coverful partners with global merchants and artisans in order to provide you with the highest quality products at the best possible prices.

We deal with suppliers located in the USA, China and Australia. Our global suppliers provide tracking numbers and we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Your order is tracked from production until delivery, at no extra cost to you.

Because we ship directly from our partners in these countries, your item may take 10 to 20 days to arrive.



What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept Credit Cards, Debit Cards, PayPal and Bank Transfer (through PayPal).
